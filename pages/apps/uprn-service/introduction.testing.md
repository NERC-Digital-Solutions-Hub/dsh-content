# DSH UPRN Service

Welcome to the **UPRN App beta**. This app brings together **UPRNs**, **environmental data**, and **geographical data**, making it easier to find and download the data you need.

It is designed to help users quickly define **areas of interest**, explore what data is available for those areas, and generate downloads for use in **GIS**, **research**, **planning**, and **analysis**. The interactive map helps preview coverage, while the app streamlines access to multiple datasets through a simple guided workflow.

Some content is still being expanded during beta testing.
