# ✨ Welcome to the NERC DSH User Testing Site

Thank you for participating in the user testing of the **NERC Digital Solutions Hub (DSH) Data Catalogue** and the **UPRN Service**.  
This site has been created to help us gather feedback, understand your experience, and improve these services for the broader community.

---

## What this is

This is a **user testing site** for:

- **Data Catalogue** – navigating large volumes of documentation can be a significant challenge. To address this, we’ve developed an AI-powered system that processes and analyses a collection of publicly available support documents, making the information within them easier to access and understand.
- **UPRN Service** – this service allows users to extract a wide range of environmental datasets attached to Unique Property Reference Numbers (UPRNs). The primary purpose of this service is to download UPRNs tagged with environmental indicators. You then import this data into your own software for further analysis.

This testing environment allows you to explore functionality and features ahead of the official launch.

---

## What we want you to do

As a tester, we’re interested in:

- **Explore the interfaces**  
  Is the system easy to use and understand?

- **Try typical workflows**  
  Are you able to do what we asked you to do?

- **Observe usability & clarity**  
  Does the interface make sense? Are results intuitive?

- **Report your experience**  
  Tell us what works, what doesn’t, and what to improve.

---

## Focus areas for testing

Here are some key areas we’re especially interested in:

- **Search effectiveness** – how well can you find relevant datasets?
- **Result clarity** – are descriptions, tags, and categories clear and useful?
- **Overall usability** – how easy or difficult is it to complete tasks?

---

## How to provide feedback

Please use our feedback mechanism to report:

- Any bugs or unexpected behaviour
- Confusing wording or unclear instructions
- Suggestions for improvement
- Tasks you couldn’t complete
